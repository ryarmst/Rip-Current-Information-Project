For Schools & Educators

Free water safety education connected to places students know.

RiP offers learning opportunities designed to support educators and enhance existing curriculum without adding extra burden.

What We Offer

  • In-person classroom presentations.
  • On-site beach learning experiences where appropriate and weather-dependent.
  • Age-appropriate lessons for elementary and secondary students.
  • Materials that complement programs such as Swim to Survive.

How a Visit Works

  • You share your school's needs, grade level, and preferred format.
  • RiP tailors the session to your students and location.
  • The presentation is delivered in your classroom or at a nearby beach.
  • Students leave with a clear understanding of water safety and rip current awareness.

Curriculum Connections

  • Personal safety and injury prevention.
  • Environmental awareness and systems.
  • Decision-making and risk assessment.
  • Physical literacy and water safety fundamentals.

Book a Visit

There is no cost for schools.

Our goal is to remove barriers and ensure water safety education is available to all students. Sessions can range from short assemblies to longer, hands-on learning experiences.
