What We Do

Clear safety information for real Great Lakes conditions.

Education

Age-appropriate water safety lessons help students and families recognize risk before entering the water.

Presentations

Founder Nathan MacIntyre delivers practical, evidence-informed presentations for schools, municipalities, rescue agencies, and community groups.

Community

RiP works with local partners so safety messaging is visible, consistent, and tailored to Great Lakes communities.

Research

Our public education is grounded in coastal science and expert insight, including collaboration with Dr. Chris Houser at the University of Waterloo.
